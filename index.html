Ninja Dash

Slash your way through hordes of enemy ninjas! Rack up points, unlock weapons, and survive the dash.

About the Game

  • Core Gameplay: Top-down 2D action. Move with WASD, attack with SPACE (aims at mouse).
  • Scoring: +500 per kill, +1,000 every 10 seconds.
  • Weapons:
    • Katana (default): Melee slash.
    • Pistol (7,500 pts): Ranged shots.
    • LMG (15,000 pts): Rapid fire.
    • RPG (30,000 pts): Explosive blasts.
    • God Weapon (0.1% drop): One-shot beam (ultra-rare!).
  • Win/Lose: Win at 30,000 pts. Keep going for endless fun!
